One Man Kitchen Ordering System

A modern ordering website serving bold, spicy Sri Lankan cuisine and Asian fusion from Kurunegala—freshly cooked, hot, and just a click away.

Overview

One Man Kitchen is a modern digital ordering and reservation platform built for a bold, fast-moving food brand specializing in spicy Sri Lankan cuisine and Asian fusion dishes. Based in Kurunegala, the system enables customers to browse the menu, place orders, and make reservations directly through WhatsApp.

The goal was to replace a fully manual communication process with a structured, mobile-first digital experience that improves speed, clarity, and customer convenience.

Problem

The restaurant relied entirely on manual WhatsApp messages, calls, and walk-ins for both orders and reservations, with no structured system in place.

This led to unclear communication, missed requests, and difficulty managing peak-time demand efficiently.

There was no centralized menu or booking flow, making the process inconsistent and time-consuming for both customers and staff.

Goals

  • Build a fast mobile-first ordering & reservation system
  • Centralize menu and booking flow
  • Reduce manual communication errors
  • Improve customer experience via WhatsApp integration
  • Support scalable digital growth

Solution

A structured mobile-first website was developed where users can browse the menu, place orders, and make reservations through WhatsApp with pre-filled messages. This removes friction from communication while maintaining the simplicity of WhatsApp as the core interaction channel.

The system organizes all customer interactions into a clear flow, improving speed, reducing errors, and making operations significantly more efficient.

Design System

The system was designed for clarity, speed, and mobile-first usability, ensuring users can quickly browse, order, or reserve without unnecessary steps.

UI/UX Approach

Minimal navigation, category-based menu structure, and a direct WhatsApp action flow ensure users can complete actions in seconds.

Built on a lightweight Vite system optimized for mobile performance, delivering instant load times and a seamless user experience.

Design Elements
