Our Story

Zoura Labs was founded by Madhushan Hemachandra, a software engineer, with a simple vision: to create a digital agency that values quality, innovation, and long-term partnerships over quick wins.

Established in 2025, Zoura Labs has grown into a full-service digital agency, helping businesses build websites, mobile applications, custom software, AI-powered solutions, and digital experiences that create real business value.

From the beginning, our focus has been on combining thoughtful design, strong engineering, and strategic thinking to deliver digital solutions that are scalable, reliable, and built around our clients' goals.

Founder Quote

"At Zoura Labs, we believe modern businesses deserve solutions that are not only technically sound but also human‑centered. Every project is a chance to build trust and drive measurable impact."
— Madhushan Founder & CEO

Our Philosophy

At Zoura Labs, we don't see digital products as one-time deliverables. We see them as long-term business assets that should streamline operations, create meaningful customer experiences, and support sustainable growth.

Our mission is simple: to build digital solutions that solve real problems while raising the standard of digital experiences. We're committed to becoming one of Sri Lanka's leading digital product studios—one project, one partnership, and one success story at a time.

Where We Work

Based in Colombo, Sri Lanka, Zoura Labs partners with startups, growing businesses, established brands, and organizations looking to build, improve, and scale their digital presence.

While we're proudly based in Sri Lanka, we collaborate with clients around the world, delivering websites, mobile apps, custom software, AI-powered solutions, and digital strategies with the same commitment to quality, communication, and long-term success.

What We Do

We help businesses build, launch, and grow through modern digital solutions. Our expertise spans web design, UI/UX, custom websites, e-commerce platforms, mobile and desktop applications, custom software, AI automation, SEO, branding, and ongoing support.

Every project is built with a product mindset—focusing on user experience, performance, scalability, and long-term business value. Whether we're developing a marketing website, an e-commerce platform, or a complex business system, our goal is to deliver solutions that are reliable, intuitive, and built for growth.

We work with modern technologies including Next.js, React, TypeScript, Node.js, Shopify, WordPress, Framer, and Webflow, choosing the right tools for each project's goals rather than a one-size-fits-all approach.

Industries We Serve

We work across e-commerce, education, healthcare, SaaS, hospitality, and service-based industries. Each solution is tailored to real-world needs—whether it’s increasing conversions, improving user experience, or building scalable digital platforms.

Our approach is always the same: understand the business first, then design and build systems that support growth, efficiency, and long-term success.

Collaborative Studio

The Team Behind the Work

While Zoura Labs is founded and led by Madhushan Hemachandra, every project benefits from the expertise of a close-knit team of designers, developers, and digital specialists who collaborate throughout the entire process.

Rather than working in isolated departments, we bring together design, development, strategy, and technology from the start. This collaborative approach allows us to solve problems faster, make better decisions, and create digital products that are both functional and thoughtfully designed.

Every project receives hands-on attention, with each team member contributing where their expertise adds the most value. From discovery and design to development, testing, and launch, we work together to ensure every solution meets the highest standards of quality, performance, and user experience.

By staying intentionally lean and collaborative, Zoura Labs remains agile enough to adapt quickly while delivering websites, mobile apps, custom software, AI solutions, and digital experiences that businesses can rely on as they grow.
